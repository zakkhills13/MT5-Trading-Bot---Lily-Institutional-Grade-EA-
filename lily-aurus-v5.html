Directional Intelligence

The Four Pillars of the Engine

Lily Aurus V5 uses a four-component scoring system combining independent market signals.

Pillar 1: Volume Profile Mean-Reversion (The Heartbeat)

+

Imagine looking at the last 100 candles and asking: "Where did the most trading happen?" That is the Point of Control (POC) — the market's fair value. Around the POC is the Value Area, where ~70% of recent trading occurred.

The edge: When price pushes to the Value Area High (VAH), big money sells because it's seen as expensive. When price drops to the Value Area Low (VAL), big money buys. Lily Aurus trades this phenomenon—selling near VAH, buying near VAL, targeting the POC. Each signal is further confirmed by real-time Delta Volume analysis to assure flow direction.

Pillar 2: DXY Correlation Filter (The Dollar Compass)

+

Gold and the USD feature an inverse relationship. Lily Aurus V5 reads the DXY (US Dollar Index) in real-time utilizing a Volume-Weighted Rate of Change. It gauges the conviction tied to Dollar shifts to either confirm or block a Gold-trade setup, preventing numerous bad entries per month.

(If your broker does not carry a DXY feed, the EA adjusts its scoring dynamically without crashing.)

Pillar 3: EMA Crossover — Session Momentum

+

Uses EMA5 and EMA20 on the hourly timeframe. EMA5 serves as the short-term burst, EMA20 acts as the session anchor. The EA only enters buy trades during bullish sessions and sells during bearish sessions, unless a massive Volume Profile mean-reversion extreme forcibly bypasses this rule.

Pillar 4: RSI Momentum Confirmation

+

Utilizes RSI to assess directional exhaustion (70-80 = overbought, 20-30 = oversold). This helps the EA avoid jumping into buys when the market is totally maxed out upside, or sells when heavily dumped down.

Active Protection

The Guardian System

Once a trade is open, a dedicated engine continuously monitors its health — every tick, watching for signs that the market has changed its mind.

Guardian Q1 — Signal Reversal Exit

When you enter a trade, the EA records a "DNA snapshot" — the VP levels, DXY reading, EMA state, RSI, and signal strength. As the trade runs, Q1 continuously monitors whether the original reasons are still valid:

  • Has price broken outside the Value Area in the wrong direction?
  • Has the DXY reversed and is now working against the trade?
  • Has the EMA flipped against the position?

If two or more of these signals flip, and the move shows sustained wrong-way momentum while in profit, Q1 exits the trade early — the automated equivalent of "The reason I entered is gone — I'm out." It also shifts to defensive modes if a trade runs for an unrealistic time duration.

Guardian Q2 — Floating P&L Protection

Tracks daily exposure combining closed P&L and floating positions across three escalating alert levels:

Level Trigger Action
WARNING 80% of daily loss limit New trades blocked
DANGER 95% of daily loss limit All trails tightened aggressively
BREACH 100% of daily loss limit All positions closed, trading halted

Q2 also monitors the profit side: when daily floating profit approaches target, it tightens all trailing stops to lock in gains. Daily P&L state is saved to file — so if the EA restarts, it picks up exactly where it left off.

Profit Mechanics

Intelligent Exits & Profit Locking

Static pip-based stops are a relic of the past. Every exit parameter adapts to the market's actual volatility.

📐

ATR-Adaptive Stop Loss & Take Profit

Stop Loss: ATR × 1.75 — gives the trade enough room to breathe without being too far away.

Take Profit: ATR × 5.0 — targets approximately a 2.5:1 reward-to-risk ratio.

Both have floor and ceiling limits based on configured pip values, so they never go unreasonably tight or wide. Stops widen organically during news shocks and tighten during consolidations.

🎯

Dynamic Partial Close at POC

When the trade reaches the Point of Control (POC) — the market's fair value and the EA's primary target — it automatically closes 40% of the position to lock in profit.

After the partial close, the stop loss is moved to breakeven, making the remaining 60% completely risk-free. If POC hasn't been reached, a fallback at 150 pips of profit triggers the partial.

Structure-Aware Trailing Stop

Not a fixed-pip trail — an intelligent one that reads market structure.

HVN-Based Trailing

Once a trade reaches the configured profit threshold (default: 100 pips), the trailing stop activates. The EA scans the Volume Profile for High Volume Nodes (HVN) — price levels where large volumes previously traded — and trails just below/above them. These act as natural structural barricades.

Graceful Fallback

If no suitable HVN is found in the current profile, the system falls back to a standard fixed-pip trail (default: 70 pips behind price).

Guardian Override

When Guardian Q2 enters DANGER mode, all trailing distances are aggressively tightened — overriding normal trail logic to protect daily limits.

Execution Ecosystem

Intelligent Risk & Regimes

Lily adjusts sizing and behavior dynamically based on real-time equity patterns and market states.

Loss Streak Decay

2 losses forces lots to 75%. 3-4 drops to 50%. 5+ halts trading. Losses over 48h old only carry half the weight, allowing recovery.

ATR Scaling

Vol spikes >1.5x cuts lots to 75%. Spikes >2.0x slices to 50% limiting exposure during erratic news bursts.

Equity Curve Protection

Tracks a running EMA of recent percentage returns. If short-term performance is weaker than the longer-term trend (equity curve pointing down), lot sizes are reduced to 75% until performance recovers.

Final Lot Multiplier

All three factors combine into a single multiplier applied to every trade — automatically, in real time. No manual intervention needed.

Market Regime Processing (ADX)

  • Ranging (ADX < 35): Full normal mean-reversion active. This is where the system works best.
  • Trending (ADX 35-50): Requires STRONG signals. Lots to 75%. SL widened slightly.
  • Volatile (ADX > 50): Entries paused. Prevents mean-reverting into a massive trending move — one of the most common causes of losses.

Trade Management Targets

Smart SL & TP: Calculated via ATR (Default SL: 1.75x ATR, TP: 5.0x ATR) targeting ~2.5:1 R/R ratio. You only need to win 1 out of every 3.5 trades to be profitable.

Structure-Aware Trails: Trails tuck directly behind Volume Profile High Volume Nodes (HVNs). Enacts 40% partial closes exactly at the Point of Control (POC).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does it trade anything besides Gold?
A: Engineered EXCLUSIVELY for XAUUSD. VP modeling and session blocks assume Gold liquidity characteristics.
Q: What timeframe chart should I use?
A: Any. The EA independently pulls H1 and H4 data required for its logic internally. The chart timeframe you attach it to does not affect operation.
Q: Martingale or Grid?
A: Absolutely not. Every trade possesses fixed balance-backed SL and discrete standalone sizing. No position doubling, no averaging down, no grid of orders.
Q: If VPS restarts mid-day?
A: EA regenerates its true-daily state analyzing past executed broker history and restoring P&L cache maps natively. Open positions are detected and their DNA is reconstructed. No amnesia.
Q: Will it work on a demo account?
A: Yes, and highly recommended. Run it on a demo account matching your prop firm's balance for at least 2–4 weeks to observe behavior before live deployment.
Q: What if my broker has no DXY feed?
A: The EA detects this automatically and redistributes signal weight to the remaining three components. You lose one filter layer, but the core VP + EMA + RSI engine still functions fully.
Q: Can I run multiple instances?
A: Each instance must have a unique Magic Number. Since Lily Aurus V5 is Gold-specific, running multiple instances on Gold simultaneously is not recommended.
